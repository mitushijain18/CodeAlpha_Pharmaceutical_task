# Literature Review - Pharmaceutical Research Task

## Project Overview

This project is completed as part of the CodeAlpha Pharmaceutical Internship. The task focuses on conducting a literature review to analyze existing research studies, scientific articles, and publications related to pharmaceutical science and healthcare advancements.

The main objective of this project is to study previous research work, understand current developments in the pharmaceutical field, and summarize important findings from scientific literature.

## About Literature Review

A literature review is a systematic analysis of existing research and published information on a specific topic. It helps in understanding previous discoveries, identifying research trends, and finding gaps for future studies.

In the pharmaceutical field, literature reviews play an important role in understanding drug development, treatment approaches, clinical studies, and healthcare innovations.

The main objectives of this literature review are:
